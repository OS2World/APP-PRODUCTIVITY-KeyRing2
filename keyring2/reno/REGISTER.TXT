 บฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑ  What you need to register  ฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑบ
 ศออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออผ

   If you wish to become a registered KeyRing/2 user, all you need do is make 
   a payment to the author, via one of the local registration sites listed
   in the registration form. Many registration sites will accept credit
   cards, all will accept cash or cashier's check.

   Please see the file ORDER.FRM for details and pricing.

 บฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑ  Registration procedure  ฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑฑบ
 ศออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออออผ

   Upon receipt of your registration fee with the completed registration
   form you will receive a Registration Key which will correspond to the
   registration string given in the registration form.

   Upon receipt of your registration key you should start KeyRing/2
   with no command line parameters, and click on the Help menu item.

   Next, use the mouse to click on the Product Information menu item.

   Type in your First and Last Name EXACTLY as found on your Registration 
   Key form.

   Type in the Serial Number and Registration number from the
   Registration Key form.
   
   If you typed in all the fields correctly, KeyRing/2 will add a
   small entry to your OS2.INI file to hold the current registration
   info.  You are now registered, and can access all the features of
   KeyRing/2!

   We strongly suggest you put a copy your Registration Key
   information in a safe place.  If your OS2.INI file is tweaked,
   restored from an old backup, or if you reinstall OS/2, it is possible 
   for KeyRing/2 to revert to "Demo" mode, with certain limitations
   on use.

   It is possible, depending upon the facilities available at your local
   registration site, to have the key delivered to your computer system,
   to have it made available for pickup by you or to have it delivered
   by your local postal service.
